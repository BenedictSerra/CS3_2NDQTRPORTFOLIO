What is Clash Royale?

Clash Royale is a real-time, free to play, multiplayer strategy game developed by Supercell. In this game, players collect and upgrade different cards — some being from Clash of Clans — in order to form a deck and battle opponents in real time.

Trophies

Whenever you win in Clash Royale, you gain a certain amount of trophies depending on your arena. As you climb the trophy ladder, you unlock higher arenas. In these arenas, you obtain new cards that you can add to your deck in order to make it better.
*It is important to note that even if a card is obtained in a higher arena, it isnt always better.

Decks

In Clash Royale, decks are a set of 8 cards that a player chooses to use in battle. This can be a mix of troops, spells and buildings and there are no restrictions to which cards you put together. This means there is an infinite amount of possible decks, and it is up to the player to strategize and optimize their deck. These decks can be grouped into different types based on how they are played. Let's take a look at the most common ones!

Average Elixir Cycle
